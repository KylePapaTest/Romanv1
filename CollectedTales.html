Hercules

Son of Jupiter

Hercules Was A Demigod (Demi Means Half In Latin!).
His Mother Was Human, His Father Was Jupiter King Of The Gods!
Hercules Lived On Earth, He Grew To Be Extremely Strong!

Hercules Was Given Tasks By Someone Who Could See The Future, If Completed He Would Live Forever! The Tasks Had Been Designed To Be Impossible, But Hercules Always Found A Way!

The First Tale Is Of The Nemean Lion His First Task, This Lion Had A Golden Coat Impervious To Weapons! It Hunted The Weak Taking Them To A Cave As Bait For The Brave!

Hercules Was Approached By A Man Whose Son Had Been Taken, Hercules Rushed To The Rescue But Soon Realised His Weapons Failed To Damage The Lion!

Improvising Quickly, Hercules Waited For The Lion To Slash With Its Golden Claws! Sidestepping At The Last Second He Managed To Hold The Lion In A Headlock Using His Strength To Break Its Neck!

Hercules Saved The Young Boy Then Used The Lions Golden Claws To Take The Golden Fur From The Lion, Adding It To His Armour Making Him Near Invincible!
