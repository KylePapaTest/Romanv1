Mars

Mars also known as Ares in Greek mythology is often shown in decorative armour, it is said that Mars would later father both Romulus and Remus.

Romans often prayed to Mars before battles, they believed he would protect them.

Mar's symbol was the Ancile, a sacred shield that legend has it would keep rome safe so long as it stayed within the city.
