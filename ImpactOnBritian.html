Life Styles
Moreview_headline

The Romans Lived Various Lifestyles Depending on Wealth and Status.

Life Stylesclose

Wealthy Romans lived in beautiful houses and experienced an extravagant lifestyle with luxurys such as being surrounded by servants and slaves to cater to their every desire.

Poorer Romans could only dream of such a life. They lived in shabby houses that could collapse at any moment.
